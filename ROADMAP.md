Roadmap

Phase 0 — Foundations (1–2 weeks)
- Repo scaffolding (monorepo), CI (lint/tests), docker‑compose env.
- Auth basics: register/login/logout, email verification.
- Profiles: create/update/view profile.

Phase 1 — Listings & Feed (2–3 weeks)
- Items CRUD with presigned uploads and image variants.
- Feed (chrono + followed sellers), item detail, favorites/likes.
- Search & filters (Postgres FTS); indexing and pagination.

Phase 2 — Social & Notifications (2 weeks)
- Follows, comments, in‑app notifications.
- Reporting and basic admin dashboard.

Phase 3 — Messaging (2–3 weeks)
- Buyer‑seller threads; WebSocket or server‑sent events.
- Notification push channel.

Phase 4 — Offers & Payments (3–4 weeks)
- Offers with accept/counter/decline.
- Payment integration (Stripe) and basic order state.

Phase 5 — Scale & Polish (ongoing)
- CDN, caching strategies, observability, moderation automation, recommendation MVP.

Milestones & KPIs
- M1: Users can list and interact (DAU, listings/day, favorites/day).
- M2: Social loops (follows, comments, notifications) — retention lift.
- M3: Conversations and offers — conversion to transactions.
